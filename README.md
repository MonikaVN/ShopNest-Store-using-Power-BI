## ShopNest-Store-using-Power-BI

## Overview

This project focuses on analysing the sales performance of ShopNest Store using Power BI. The main objective was to explore customer purchasing behaviour, delivery performance, revenue trends, and regional demand patterns. By transforming raw transactional datasets into meaningful visual insights, the project helps businesses understand key performance indicators and support data-driven decision-making. The dashboard enables interactive analysis of sales, logistics efficiency, and customer satisfaction metrics.

## Key Features

Identified top product categories by total sales revenue to understand profitable business segments.
Performed delayed delivery analysis to detect logistics and supply-chain performance issues.
Compared monthly on-time vs delayed orders using drill-through capability for detailed order inspection.
Analysed customer payment method preferences to improve payment convenience and service strategy.
Evaluated product ratings to identify high-performing and low-performing products based on customer satisfaction.
Conducted state-wise sales analysis to understand regional demand variations.
Studied seasonal and quarterly sales trends for demand forecasting and promotional planning.
Analysed year-wise revenue growth to evaluate overall business performance and profitability trends.
